The Best Movies According to Tony

My top 3 movies of all-time!

How to Train Your Dragon (2010)

A cartoon ahead of it's time, especially when he first rides the dragon.

Race to Witch Mountain (2009)

Has the Rock in it, and the kids have powers, what more do you want?

How to Fall in Love (2012)

Nice love story of a dorky guy that learns how to be cool.
